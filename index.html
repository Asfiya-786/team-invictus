FAQs

  • 💳 What documents are required for account registration?

    You will need a valid government ID proof, address proof, and basic personal details to complete the registration and KYC verification process.

  • ⏳ How long does account verification take?

    Account verification usually takes 24–48 hours after successful submission of all required documents.

  • 💰 Can I apply for a loan online?

    Yes. You can apply for loans directly through our online portal by filling out the loan application form and submitting required financial details.

  • 📉 What affects my loan approval?

    Loan approval depends on factors such as credit score, income stability, existing debts, and overall risk assessment results.

  • 🔄 Can I update my personal details later?

    Yes. You can update certain personal details through your account settings after logging in. Some sensitive changes may require re-verification.

  • 📱 Is mobile banking available?

    Yes. Our banking services are fully responsive and can be accessed on mobile devices, tablets, and desktops.

  • 🛡️ How do you protect customer data?

    We use multi-layer security, encryption, and secure authentication protocols to ensure all customer data remains protected at all times.

  • 💹 What are your standard loan interest rates?

    Our loan interest rates are highly competitive, starting from as low as 6.5% p.a. depending on the loan category, tenure, and credit score evaluation.
